---
title: Spark
date: '2025-06-16'
---

And I saw the spark, <br>
the one whose chase I'd given up, <br>
and I stole it off the night's hands <br>
and I swallowed it whole.

And I thought of how <br>
it would've fallen and grown <br>
and I opened up my body <br>
and I combed through the ashes <br>
and saw the dark but not the flicker.
